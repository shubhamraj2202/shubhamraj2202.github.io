About Hair Lens - AI

Most hairstyle preview apps fail the same way — the result looks like a bad filter or a wig dropped on your head. Hair Lens - AI was built around a single obsession: keeping your face exactly as it is while transforming only the hair. The result is a photorealistic preview you can actually bring to your barber or salon.

Built natively for iOS with SwiftUI and powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash Image, the entire generation pipeline runs through a Cloudflare Worker proxy — so your API keys never touch the device and your photos are never retained after the response is delivered.
