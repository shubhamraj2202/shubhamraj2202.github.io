Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026

1. Data Collection Overview

Hair Lens - AI is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect only the minimal data necessary to provide our AI hairstyle preview service.

  • Photos (selfies) you upload are sent to our API for AI-powered hairstyle generation and are not retained after the response is returned
  • Purchase receipts are processed locally via Apple StoreKit 2 solely for credit validation
  • A device identifier is used for rate limiting to prevent abuse — it is not linked to any personal information
  • No account or login is required to use the app
  • No personally identifiable information is collected beyond what is described above

2. How We Use Your Photos

When you upload a selfie, it is transmitted securely to our server and processed by our AI service for hairstyle generation. The AI modifies only the hair region while preserving your face.

  • Photos are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers
  • Generated preview images are returned directly to your device
  • We do not use your photos for training AI models
  • We do not share your photos with any third party other than our AI processing service for the sole purpose of processing your request

3. Third-Party Services

Hair Lens - AI integrates with the following third-party services:

  • AI Processing Service: Used for AI-powered hairstyle image generation. Images are processed by our AI provider and are not retained.
  • Apple StoreKit 2: Handles all in-app purchase transactions for credits. Payment data is managed entirely by Apple and is never seen by Hair Lens - AI.
  • Cloudflare Workers: Our serverless API infrastructure processes requests and applies rate limiting using your device identifier.

4. In-App Purchases & Credits

Hair Lens - AI uses a credit-based system. Credits are purchased through Apple's in-app purchase system (StoreKit 2). We receive only a purchase receipt to validate and credit your account — no payment card or financial details are accessible to us.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately. Please contact us if you believe this has occurred.

6. Data Security

All photo uploads are transmitted over HTTPS. We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

Hair Lens - AI does not retain photos on our servers after processing is complete. Credit balances and device identifiers used for rate limiting are stored only as long as necessary to operate the service. You can remove all local app data by deleting the app from your device.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we collect (as described in this policy)
  • Request deletion of any data associated with your device identifier
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns or questions

9. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
