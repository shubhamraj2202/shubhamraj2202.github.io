Hair Lens - AI is a personal hairstyle preview application that lets you upload a selfie, choose from a curated catalog of hairstyles or enter a custom prompt, and receive an AI-generated preview of yourself with that hairstyle. Only the hair region is modified — your face is preserved. The app is intended for personal, entertainment, and inspiration purposes only.
